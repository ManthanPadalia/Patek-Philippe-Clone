5968A-001 - AQUANAUT

SELF-WINDING

                    WATCH:  Self-winding mechanical movement. Caliber CH 28‑520 C.
                            Flyback chronograph. Central chronograph hand. 
                            60-minute counter. Date in an aperture

                    DIAL:   Black embossed, gold applied numerals with luminescent coating.
                    
                    CASE:   Steel. Screw-down crown. Sapphire crystal case back. 
                            Water-resistant to 120 m. 
                            Diameter (10-4 o’clock): 42.2 mm. Height: 11.9 mm.
                    
                    Strap: Composite material, black (originally fitted). 
                            Composite material, orange (additional strap). 
                            Aquanaut fold-over clasp.                                     
                        
                            The sporty and very contemporary Aquanaut collection 
                            welcomes its first chronograph – a complication that 
                            perfectly matches the character of this youthful and 
                            dynamic timepiece line. Endowed with a self-winding 
                            flyback chronograph movement, the Reference 5968 also 
                            stands out with remarkable aesthetic appeal, typified by 
                            orange chronograph displays contrasting against the black 
                            dial. It comes with two straps: one orange, one black, in 
                            an ultra-resistant composite material.
